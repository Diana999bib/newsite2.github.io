About Marilyn Monroe

Marilyn Monroe (1926–1962) was an American actress, model, and singer. She became one of the most famous Hollywood icons of the 20th century and is remembered for her beauty, talent, and charisma.

Life & Work

  1. 1926 – Born in Los Angeles, California
  2. 1950s– Rose to fame as a Hollywood star
  3. 1953 – Starred in "Gentlemen Prefer Blondes"
  4. 1955 – Founded her own film production company
  5. 1962 – Passed away at age 36
  • Hollywood Icon
  • Fashion Influence
  • Successful Actress and Singer
